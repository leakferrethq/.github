# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
leakferret project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for our mistakes and learning from them
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community

Behavior that is not acceptable includes any conduct a reasonable person would
consider inappropriate in a professional setting: personal attacks, public or
private harassment, deliberate intimidation, or publishing others' private
information without their explicit permission.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (repositories, issues, pull
requests, discussions) and when an individual is representing the project in
public spaces.

## Adoption and full text

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant][homepage], version 2.1. The
complete text, including the enforcement guidelines and the escalation ladder,
is the canonical reference for this Code and is available at:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project
maintainer at **missusk@protonmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainer is obligated to respect the
privacy and security of anyone who reports an incident.
